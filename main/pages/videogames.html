Video Games

  1. Bioshock Infinite

    Reasons:
    The gameplay is very entertaining, especially with the vigors that give you very unique abilities. What stands out the most, however, is the incredible story.

  2. Undertale

    Reasons:
    From the music, to the story, to the gameplay, this game is incredible. You can choose to befriend the characters and get attatched to them or you can kill them all, and face the consequences.

  3. Terraria

    Reasons:
    This game involves a lot of grinding, but it remains fun due to the very diverse and challenging bosses. You can also get really creative. I would like it if the game had more lore, though.

  4. Minecraft

    Reasons:
    An incredible sandbox. You can do whatever you want to do. Minecraft is also a beautiful canvas where you can build incredible things. But if you're looking for a challange, you can go kill the enderdragon.

  5. Finding Paradise

    Reasons:
    This game is no more than an interactive story. It could've been written as a book... but it is a game, and I honestly loved it and its music.
